# Boat Owner Maintenance Pack — Draft Product Content

## Product Description
Keep your boat organized from spring launch to winterization. This printable bundle gives boat owners one place to track maintenance, repairs, fuel, batteries, safety gear, trailer needs, marina costs, and annual expenses.

## Included Templates

### 1. Boat Profile
- Boat name
- Make/model/year
- Hull ID
- Registration number
- Engine make/model/serial
- Prop size
- Battery types/dates
- Trailer VIN/license
- Marina/slip info
- Insurance contact

### 2. Pre-Launch Checklist
- Registration/current documents onboard
- Drain plug installed
- Battery charged/tested
- Bilge pump tested
- Navigation lights tested
- Fuel level checked
- Oil/fluids checked
- Prop inspected
- Safety gear onboard
- Fire extinguisher charged
- Life jackets counted/inspected
- Lines/fenders onboard
- Weather checked

### 3. Post-Trip Checklist
- Trash removed
- Fuel noted
- Battery switched/charging
- Bilge checked
- Cover secured
- Lines/fenders secure
- Issues logged

### 4. Winterization Checklist
- Fuel stabilized
- Engine flushed
- Oil changed
- Lower unit checked
- Water systems drained
- Batteries removed/maintained
- Interior dried/cleaned
- Cover inspected
- Pest prevention
- Insurance/storage confirmed

### 5. Service Log

### 7. Repair Log

### 8. Battery & Electrical Log

### 9. Safety Gear Checklist

### 10. Annual Cost Summary
