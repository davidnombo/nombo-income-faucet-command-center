# Family History Research Starter Kit — Draft Product Content

## Product Description
A printable genealogy worksheet bundle for organizing ancestor research, sources, family interviews, cemetery visits, timelines, and biography drafts.

## Included Templates

### 1. Ancestor Profile
Full name | Birth | Marriage | Death | Burial | Parents | Spouse(s) | Children | Residences | Occupations | Military | Religion/church | Notes

### 2. Research Question Planner
Question | Known facts | Hypotheses | Records to search | Repositories/websites | Priority | Result

### 3. Source Tracker
Source title | Repository/URL | Date accessed | Person/event | Evidence found | Citation notes | Reliability

### 4. Timeline Builder
Date | Age | Place | Event | Source | Notes/conflicts

### 5. Census Extraction Worksheet
Year | Location | Household members | Ages | Occupations | Neighbors | Clues | Questions

### 6. Cemetery Visit Log
Cemetery | GPS/address | Person | Grave location | Inscription | Nearby graves | Photos taken | Follow-up

### 7. Obituary Extraction Worksheet
Name | Date/place death | Survivors | Predeceased | Funeral home | Burial | Organizations | Clues

### 8. Family Interview Guide
Childhood | Parents/grandparents | Homes | School | Work | Military | Church | Traditions | Photos | Stories | Unknowns

### 9. DNA Match Notes
Match name | cM | Shared matches | Probable line | Contact status | Evidence | Next step

### 10. Family Biography Outline
Opening scene | Chronology | Places | Work | Family | Hardships | Historical context | Sources | Unresolved questions
